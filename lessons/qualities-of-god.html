Qualities of God

Introduction

God is our Creator, Provider, and Protector. He is a spirit who made all things and continues to care for His creation. Learning about the qualities of God helps us understand His nature and how we should live to please Him.

Main Qualities of God

  • Omnipotent (All-Powerful): God has unlimited power. He created the universe and everything in it.
  • Omniscient (All-Knowing): God knows everything that has happened, is happening, and will happen.
  • Omnipresent (Everywhere): God is present in all places at all times.
  • Holy: God is pure, perfect, and without sin.
  • Loving and Merciful: God loves all His creation and forgives those who repent.
  • Just: God is fair in all His dealings and rewards or punishes according to our actions.

What We Learn

  • We should trust God’s power and depend on Him in every situation.
  • We should strive to be honest, loving, and forgiving like God.
  • We must worship God for His greatness and thank Him for His care.

Assessment Questions

  1. Who is God?
  2. List five qualities of God you have learned today.
  3. What does it mean that God is omnipresent?
  4. Why should we be grateful to God?
  5. How can we show that we love God?
