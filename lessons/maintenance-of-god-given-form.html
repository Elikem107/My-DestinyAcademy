Religious and Moral Education

Strand: God and Worship

Sub-strand: Maintenance Of God's Creation

Introduction

God created everyone in a unique and beautiful way. Our form (shape and structure) and color (skin tone) are part of God’s wonderful design. We must learn to appreciate and take care of our bodies the way God made them, rather than trying to change ourselves to look like others.

Main Ideas

  • God made human beings in His image and likeness — that makes everyone special.
  • Our skin color, height, and features are all part of God’s perfect creation.
  • We must keep our bodies clean and healthy to honor God.
  • We should never look down on others because of their appearance.
  • True beauty comes from a clean heart and good behavior, not from outward looks.

What We Learn

  • Every human being is beautiful in the way God created them.
  • We should be grateful for our body and take good care of it.
  • We must avoid practices that damage or disrespect our bodies.
  • We should love and respect others regardless of their color or form.

Assessment Questions

  1. What does “form and color” mean in God’s creation of man?
  2. List three ways we can take care of our bodies.
  3. Why should we not look down on others because of their skin color?
  4. State two reasons why we must maintain our God-given form.
  5. How can we show that we are thankful for the way God made us?
